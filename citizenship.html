Citizenship Renunciation

Important Information

Renouncing ToadVille Bay citizenship is a serious and irrevocable decision. Please consider the following:

  • Loss of all citizenship rights
  • No automatic right of return
  • May affect property ownership
  • Cannot be reversed
Requirements:
  • Proof of other citizenship
  • No pending legal obligations
  • Tax clearance certificate
  • Personal interview

Renunciation Process

  1. Initial Consultation

    Schedule a mandatory consultation with a citizenship officer

  2. Document Submission

    Submit required documents and declaration of intent

  3. Review Period

    30-day cooling-off period for final consideration

  4. Final Declaration

    Sign the formal renunciation declaration
