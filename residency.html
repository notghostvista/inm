Residency Programs

Build Your Future in ToadVille Bay

Temporary Residency

Processing Time: 2-3 months

Your first step towards making ToadVille Bay your home

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Valid work or study visa
  • Proof of financial stability
  • Clean criminal record
  • Health insurance coverage
Duration:

Initial period of 2 years, renewable for additional 2-year periods

Apply for Temporary Residency

Permanent Residency

Processing Time: 4-6 months

Establish your permanent home in ToadVille Bay

Eligibility Criteria:
  • 3+ years of temporary residency
  • Continuous employment history
  • Tax compliance record
  • Integration into society
Benefits:
  • Indefinite stay rights
  • Access to social benefits
  • Path to citizenship
Apply for Permanent Residency
