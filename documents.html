Document Requirements and Examples

Passport Requirements

Valid Passport
  • Must be valid for at least 6 months
  • Must have at least 2 blank pages
  • Must be in good condition
  • Color scan of bio page required
Passport Photo
  • 2x2 inches (51x51 mm)
  • White background
  • Neutral facial expression
  • Taken within last 6 months

Visa Documents

Visa Application Form

Complete all fields in capital letters using black ink

Bank Statement

Last 3 months showing sufficient funds

Travel Insurance

Must cover entire stay period

Residency Documents

Proof of Address

Utility bill or rental agreement not older than 3 months

Employment Contract

Signed and stamped by employer

Police Clearance

From country of origin and current residence

Citizenship Documents

Birth Certificate

Original or certified copy with apostille

Language Certificate

Minimum B1 level required

Citizenship Test Certificate

Passing score of 75% or higher required

General Document Guidelines

  • All documents must be original or certified copies
  • Non-English documents require certified translation
  • Documents may require apostille or legalization
  • Documents should not be older than 6 months unless specified
  • Bring original documents plus two sets of copies
